Why work at KAPL?

KAPL is a small team doing BIG things! We are a company that encourages our people to be All-rounders. Essentially, we seek self-starters who can assume leadership positions and participate in our growth story.

Great international exposure opportunities to excel in diverse company functions and above all a culture that gives great emphasis to employee satisfaction. We believe that aiding our personnel in achieving their full potential helps in effectively sustaining the growth and success of our company.

Work Culture

A healthy professional work culture leads to satisfied employees and an increased productivity. We treat all our employees equally. Our employees are judged only by their work and excellence never goes unnoticed.

Recognition at KAPL

Our Recognition & Reward program for outstanding performance motivates our employees to perform at their best consistently.

About MSME Accelerate

MSME Accelerate is an ecosystem catering to the needs of Small, Medium, Micro Enterprises and Start-ups. We support companies to identify and evaluate risks unique to them and provide expert guidance on Insurance, Finance, Claims, Employee Wellness and other related matters.

We believe that each company is unique in its business, structure and environment. That’s why there is no one size that fits for all with standard offering or one click support. Our experts asses your business scenario in detail and customise the tailor made program for you. We help you in valuation of your assets and liabilities to arrive at right mixture of coverage required at the right time with entire eco system support. We are backed by Technology but not blindly run by machines !! MSME Accelerate is a consulting Product by Mititek Ventures. We don’t sell Insurance.

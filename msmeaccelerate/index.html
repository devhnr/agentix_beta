About Us

KAPL is one of India's leading manufacturers of speciality chemicals. Since inception in 1991, the company has successfully delivered products under the brand name of CRISTOL™ to diversified end uses in oil field, components for lubricant additives, polymers, water treatment, coatings, pharmaceutical, personal care and agrochemical industries.

About us

Our Journey

The inception of AgentiX was deeply rooted in the firsthand experiences of its founders - Amit, Yagnesh, and Bhakti. Amit and Yagnesh, as the driving forces behind Raghnall Insurance Broking, were acutely aware of the challenges agents encountered. They regularly witnessed agents reaching out for assistance in everything from product understanding, quotation and placement support, to client meetings. These interactions highlighted the gaping need for empowering agents with the right tools and knowledge.

Bhakti's personal journey as a life insurance agent further deepened this understanding. Her transition from being a standalone agent to helming various senior positions in insurance companies provided her with an insightful perspective on the struggles and potential solutions for agents.

This collective realization, drawn from their diverse experiences and mutual passion for enhancing the insurance sector, led to the birth of AgentiX. The aim was clear: to upgrade and equip agents with the right technology, product knowledge, and professional skills to face modern market challenges with confidence.

Our Mission & Vision

At AgentiX, our mission transcends conventional boundaries. We aim to uplift insurance agents, granting them unfettered access to state-of-the-art technology and a wide array of products. And we do this without them having to cede their business ownership to larger entities. Our vision is clear: augment insurance penetration across all business classes and bring recognition and empowerment to the agents who form the backbone of the industry

Why Choose AgentiX ?

Our uniqueness lies in empowerment. With AgentiX, agents metamorphose into tech-savvy professionals, equipped to deal with every facet of the insurance landscape. Our platform ensures they don't remain confined to the traditional trio of Life, Health, and Motor insurance. And the best part? They achieve this newfound prowess without having to compromise on their business independence. No sharing of ownership, no diluted credits just pure, unadulterated empowerment.

Embark on this transformative journey with us. Let s revolutionize the insurance domain, one agent at a time.
